I'm David Kuhns.

UX RESEARCHER

Well, hello there.

I obsess over the user experience.

I am always focused on the end user. What does he value? What does he want? How does she interact with the product? I rely on extensive research to inform my decisions for product design, always working to create an improved product that is intuitive, useful, and useable.

Days after college graduation, I was like a new born baby kicking and screaming and wondering who on earth pushed me into a world of unknowns. So, I went back to school and stayed there for the next eight years. Fortunately, some good came of it: I learned so much that my friends call me "Davipedia". Oh, and I was given the title "Dr."

I go beyond the basics.

My PhD in psychology provides me with knowledge and insight others cannot provide. I take a holistic approach to UX design to make carefully informed decisions that draw on all aspects of psychology and on all knoweldge of technology. I find joy in synthesizing information to come up with solutions.

I make products better. For everyone.
